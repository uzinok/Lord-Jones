Goodbye, dry skin.

By Andi Breitowich Published: May 7, 2022

I faced these terrible symptoms to the fullest after giving birth. Sure, I used to have nagging pains in my stomach and chest before my periods, sometimes I was irritable and even angry. PMS symptoms began to interfere with my life and had me depressed, irritated, in pain almost all the time.

All my relatives know that they best not mess with me during this very week before my period. I don’t sleep well, I’m easily get mad at others, and I can’t properly focus on my work. A while ago, I screwed up a major advertising contract just because I freaked out and didn’t go to an important meeting. I was tired and just wanted to live a full life!
